Halloween線上密室逃脫X小六英語

By Candice 16 December 2022

Happy Halloween!!

The days leading up to Halloween always coincide with midterm exams.
Therefore, this time I want to combine the content from our English textbook with Halloween-themed challenges, allowing students to review while having fun solving puzzles.
If you’re interested, keep reading!
萬聖節來臨的日子總是很接近期中考，
因此這次我想要結合英語課本裡的內容加上萬聖節的闖關元素，
讓學生邊玩解謎、邊複習，如果您也有興趣，就繼續看下去吧!

  • 教學對象 Class: 國小六年級 6th Grade

  • 教學時間 Duration: 80 min-連續兩節英文課 Two consecutive English classes

  • 版本內容: 何嘉仁E-star (2024更新：新課綱已不再使用E-star,但還是可作為活動參考喔！)

  • 單元主題 Unit Theme: 國家Country + 交通工具Transportation

  • 參考句型 & 單字 Key Phrases & Vocabulary:

    1. Where is he/she from?
      Taiwan/ Japan/ the USA / the UK, etc.
    2. How can he/she get to the park?
      by bike/ by train/ by car/ on foot, etc.
    3. Halloween-related words: pumpkin/ bat/ candy, etc.

  • 使用到的媒材 Materials Used:

    1. Canva
    2. Holiyo密逃遊戲翻轉平台 Holiyo Escape Room Platform
    3. iPad
    4. 解謎道具 puzzle props

  • 教師課前準備內容 Teacher’s Preparations Before Class:

    1. 解謎道具製作
      creation of puzzle props
    2. 謎題設計(利用Canva與Holiyo密逃遊戲翻轉平台)
      puzzle design (using Canva and Holiyo)
    3. PPT製作
      PPT preparation
    4. 向學校事先借了很多萬聖節裝扮道具
      borrowing various Halloween costumes from the school

Halloween線上密室逃脫流程(共80min)
Online Halloween Escape Room Process(Total 80 min)

引起動機 Warm Up (15-20min)

  1. 情境營造: 播放PPT、簡單向學生說明活動流程，之後照排數讓學生至台前領取萬聖節裝扮道具
    Set the scene: Play the PPT and briefly explain the activity process to students.
    Then, let students come to the front to choose their Halloween costumes.

  2. 小組派代表至台前找老師領取1份任務包和平板(每人1台)
    Group leaders go to the front to receive a mission pack and an iPad (one per person). 

  3. 學生用平板掃描QR code進入Haunted House密室逃脫
    Students use the iPads to scan the QR code to enter the Haunted House escape room.

  4. 教師提醒學生活動進行間的注意事項
    Teacher reminds students of important points during the activity.

發展活動 Practice (40min )
  1. 學生開始解謎，一共有6道謎題
    Students start solving puzzles, with a total of 6 puzzles.
  2. 教師巡堂，觀察各組情形，適時給予提示與幫助
    Teacher circulates the room, observing each group’s progress and providing hints and assistance as needed. 
綜合活動 Wrap up (20min)
  1. 闖關成功小組全員舉手告訴教師，並讓平板秀出逃脫成功頁面
    Successful groups raise their hands to inform the teacher and show the escape success page on their iPads. 
  2. 全班完成後，教師邀請學生分享解題過程
    Once the whole class finishes, the teacher invites students to share their problem-solving processes.
  3. It’s Treat Time! 教師分享jelly beans，恭喜全班成功完成解謎任務
    It’s Treat Time! The teacher shares jelly beans to congratulate the whole class on successfully completing the puzzle challenge.
